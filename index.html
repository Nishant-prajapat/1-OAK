About Us

We are a Designer, Manufacturer & 100% Export house deals in all kinds of Indian handicraft furniture. We are known for Quality products & most importantly Quality services as well.

We do Wrought iron, SS, Wooden, Mango, Acacia, Teak, Reclaim, Premium Buff Leather, Antique Vintage, Retro, 1 of a kind, Textile, Accessories, Lamps & Many more.

We have Experience of good 20 Years dealing with 100s of overseas clients all over the world. We are also experts in designing unique range and customized furniture as well as beautiful finishes.
